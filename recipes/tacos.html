Ingredients

    1 (3.25 ounce) package instant mashed potatoes (such as Idahoan™ Signature Russets Mashed Potatoes) ½ cup diced onions 1 tablespoon vegetable oil salt and pepper to taste 8 corn tortillas ½ cup vegetable oil for frying, plus more as needed

Steps

    Step 1 Prepare mashed potatoes according to package directions. Step 2 Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sautee diced onions in oil until tender and translucent, 3 to 5 minutes. Add cooked mashed potatoes and season with salt and pepper. Step 3 Place tortillas on a plate. Heat in microwave until pliable, 15 to 20 seconds. Step 4 Place 1/4 cup of potato mixture in each tortilla, fold in half and secure each end with toothpicks. Step 5 Heat oil in a skillet until shimmering. Fry each taco until crispy and golden brown, about 1 minute on each side. Step 6 Remove toothpicks and top with garnishes.
